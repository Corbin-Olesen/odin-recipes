Stir fry

Description

Vegetables and chicken sauteed together in a pan with sauces

Ingredients

  • frozen chicken
  • frozen vegetable mix
  • spices
  • stir fry sauce

Steps

  1. cook chicken in pan and add spices
  2. set to the side once cooked and add vegetables to same pan
  3. cook vegetables and add spices
  4. reintroduce chicken and add stir fry sauce
  5. stir until well mixed and serve

Christmas Crack

Description

Christmas Crack is a desert of salty crackers, crunchy caramel, smooth chocolate and candy of your choice
all layered together and broken into small pieces

Ingredients

  • saltines
  • butter
  • brown sugar
  • chocolate chips
  • cereal
  • oreos

Steps

  1. line crackers in pan
  2. melt butter and brown sugar
  3. pour caramel over crackers and spread
  4. bake on 400F for 3 minutes
  5. melt chocolate chips while pan is in the oven
  6. pour chocolate over crackers and spread
  7. add oreos, cereal, or whatever toppings you chose
  8. let cool and break into smaller pieces
